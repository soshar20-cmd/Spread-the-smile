How it works

From watching to leading

You come across a Sunday Spotlight or the weekly act. No obligation — just try it once, alone, no camera.

Active

Join a pod

A personal invite into your city's group. You show up to a debrief and talk through how it actually went — including the awkward parts.

Core

Lead something

You submit an experiment, host a debrief, or curate the weekly act. The movement runs on people who were Witnesses six weeks ago.
